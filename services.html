Microdermabrasion

A wand with a diamond-encrusted tip is used to gently exfoliate your skin, revealing glowing, younger-looking skin. Microdermabrasion is an excellent treatment for diminishing the following conditions: fine lines, wrinkles, sun-damaged skin, acne and acne scars, excess oils, enlarged pores and superficial age spots. For best results treatments should be done 7-10 days apart for 6 weeks, then every 3-4 weeks to maintain the results.
