Microdermabrasion


A wand with a diamond-encrusted tip is used to gently exfoliate your skin, revealing glowing, younger-looking skin.

Microdermabrasion is an excellent treatment for diminishing the following conditions:

  • Fine lines
  • Wrinkles
  • Sun-damaged skin
  • Acne and acne scars
  • Excess oils
  • Enlarged pores
  • Superficial age spots

For best results treatments should be done 7-10 days apart for 6 weeks, then every 3-4 weeks to maintain the results.
