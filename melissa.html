Caring for others is one of my greatest passions, and providing you and your skin with relaxing, nourishing and healthy treatments is one way I can indulge in that. I became an esthetician because I want to help people fall in love with their beautiful, glowing, healthy skin. I've seen the difference just a few treatments can make and it is one of the most rewarding parts of my job. I am excited to help you experience that transformation too.

In addition to my business, I enjoy growing food in our garden and using it to make delicious, healthy meals. A few years ago I said good bye to my television so I would have more time to devote to building relationships, practicing yoga, reading and exploring the neighborhood. Each summer my boyfriend and I load up the motorcycle for an adventure to visit a new part of the country. I grew up in a small town northeast of Madison, but Madison has been my home for most of my adult life.

Whether you're struggling with serious skin issues or just want to relax and be pampered, I look forward to getting to know you and helping you meet your personal skin care goals.

– Melissa Minnig, licensed esthetician
